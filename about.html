Required Market Cap tool


With this tool you can discover the necessary market capitalization of the desired project to reach the price you want. As an example lets take Cardano and see what would be the required market cap in order to reach $25. At the moment I am writting this Cardano price is $0.4942 and its market cap is $16,892,112,294 For a $25 price, Cardano will need a market cap of $854,518,023,776 wich is Bitcoin x2 current market cap. This tool was created so it can give roughly an idea of how much growth a certain projects needs so it can reach certain price. In the above example we can see that Cardano would require a 854 Billions market cap for a $25 price. Realistically this would not happen anytime soon
